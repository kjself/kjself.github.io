Adroit Game Jam 2025 Submission: Clap Now for Helen


For my second year participating in Adroit's Game Jam through Mizzou, the theme for the year was "emotion." After thinking long and hard about how to not make "Inside Out, The Game," we settled on having the player act as the prompter/sound guy behind acclaimed talk show "Clap Now for Helen!"

There were a lot of very fun and impressive submissions to the game jam this year! We did not get to make ours as technically engaging as we'd initially hoped, but I still really love the concept and world that we came up with.
